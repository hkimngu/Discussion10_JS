Pets

Omelette

I have no idea how old he is, but he is a baby in my heart. He is yellow like an omelette hence the name. He has cute rosy cheeks, like tomatoes on an omelette.

He is a cockatiel that my cousin couldn't keep anymore, so my cousin just dropped him off at my place without telling me, and I just went home one day and found a bird in my house. I wasn't exactly looking for a pet, but I just kinda roll with it. He was an unexpected addition to my life, but it's okay, I love him.

He loves stealing. I don't know how, I don't know when, but whenever I come home there's something new that was not there before. I would come home to buttons, coins, wires that does not belong to me and I would find them in his cage. Lately it's been progressively larger items. I once came home to a flat screen TV before, so I'm not complaining.

Toast

This is Toast. He is 2 year old cat, but acts like he's 100. He is a British Shorthair cat with soft cinnamon brown fur. His nickname is Cinnamon Toast Crunch. He always looks tired, hence, why I think he's a million years old. I call him an old man, but I love him.

I found him as a kitten on the side of the road nibbling on a piece of toast. We named him after that, also that his favorite thing is toast. He's not supposed to have or eat toast, but somehow he gets it. I don't even make toast, and sometimes I don't even ahve bread in the house, but somehow when I see him, he has a slice of toast in his mouth.

He likes lounging on the couch and is very affectionate, dispite his grumpy man persona. He still very much loves toast even as an adult now. He gets grumpy and pouty when he doesn't get his way.

Butter

This is Butter. I have no idea how old she is, but she's a hamster. She is a orange-yellow-beige color. I think she might be a dwarf hamster? However, I don't know enough about hamsters to know exactly what breed she is. She is very stupid and I worry about her accidentally finding her way to the pearly gates of pet heaven, but I love her.

I wanted a hamster one day, so I got one. She loves Toast. Not the bread. Toast the Cat. Toast tolerates her, and isn't interested in eating her, thank goodness for that. Because she loves sticking with Toast, her name became Butter, since I think she would like a matching name with her best buddy.

She loves the hamster wheel and playing with Toast. She likes to bother Toast a lot while Toast naps. She like to get into shenanigans, and I worry about her accidentally wandering off to her death. She's not the brightest animal, but somehow Death spares her despite the multiple close-case scenarios.

Waffle

This is Waffle. I also have no idea how old she is, but she's a very very very old tortise. She is a sage color. She is craves violence, but I love her.

Waffle is also named after a breakfast food. I found her outside a Waffle house. She tried to bite my fingers off, and I knew I had to bring her home with me.

She's a cranky old woman and she hates me, but I love her. She likes headbutting me and trying to bite me, but I'm used to it. I sometimes wake up in the middle of the night and she's inches away from me with a knife strapped on her shell, but I want to think it's her way of expressing her love.
