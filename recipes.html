Salad

Origin: unknown

Source: TikTok

Category: Vegetarian

It is a healthy recipe when I want something filling. I found it off of TikTok and I like it.

Recipe Ingredients

  • a handful lettuce or any leafy vegetable
  • a cup of chickpeas
  • a cup of black beans
  • 1/4 cup of quinoa
  • 1/2 an avocado
  • 1/2 a cucumber
  • a sprinkle of feta
  • as much dressing of any kind as you want

Recipe Steps

  1. Boil your quinoa until ready
  2. Throw in your leafy greens in a bowl
  3. Chop your cucumbers and avocado
  4. Pour in your chickpeas, black beans, quinoa, avocado, cucumbers, and feta
  5. Mix in your dressing

Potato Nuggets

Origin: unknown

Source: TikTok

Category: Vegetarian

Sometimes I want to eat potatoes but I don't want to spend too much money. I found this recipe off of TikTok.

Recipe Ingredients

  • 2 potatoes
  • 2 Tbsp rice flour
  • 1 Tbsp corn or potato starch
  • frying oil
  • 1 Thsp chili powder

Recipe Steps

  1. Boil your potatoes until soft
  2. Mash your potatoes
  3. Mix in the rice flour and starch
  4. Form nuggets or balls out of the potatoes
  5. Fry them until crispy
  6. Dust the potatoes with chili powder

Peasant Meal (Viet. Ver.)

Origin: unknown, mixed from Asian and European cuisine

Source: Family Struggle Meal

Category: Vegetarian

Sometimes we want to go back to past and imagine ourselves as medival peasant eating a simple meal. When my family had not that much, we would just eat bread and soy sauce. It was a stuggle meal, but I love bread.

Recipe Ingredients

  • bread, ideally like a baguette or a bun
  • soy sauce for dipping
  • optional: siracha, peppers, or any spicy sauce

Recipe Steps

  1. Toast your bread. If your bread is old or hard, sprinkle a little water on it or cover it with a damp paper towel and microwave for a couple seconds, then toast it.
  2. Mix in your spicy sauce with soy sauce in a small dish
  3. Eat your bread and dip it in the soy sauce mixture
